## **VISUAL STYLE GUIDE**

### **Color Palette**:
- **Primary**: Deep purples (#6B21A8), Electric pinks (#EC4899), Neon blues (#3B82F6)
- **Accent**: Hot magentas, Electric greens, Gold highlights
- **Background**: Dark blacks, Deep blues
- **Text**: Bright whites, Neon glows, Gold accents

### **Cinematography Style**:
- **Camera Movements**: 
  - Slow-motion for emotional moments
  - Fast tracking shots for energy
  - Aerial/drone shots for scale
  - Handheld for authenticity
  - Smooth gimbal movements

- **Shot Types**:
  - Extreme close-ups (eyes, hands, details)
  - Wide establishing shots (dance floors, cityscapes)
  - Medium shots (people, interactions)
  - Low angles (powerful, aspirational)
  - High angles (crowd, scale)

### **Lighting**:
- **Neon lights**: Primary light source
- **Color washes**: Purple, pink, blue
- **Contrast**: Deep shadows, bright highlights
- **Practical lights**: Club lighting, phone screens, neon signs

### **Editing Style**:
- **Pace**: Fast cuts (0.2-0.5 sec) for energy, slow-mo for impact
- **Transitions**: 
  - Whip pans
  - Zoom transitions
  - Light leaks
  - Glitch effects
  - Smooth fades for emotional moments

- **Effects**:
  - Lens flares
  - Particle systems
  - Light trails
  - Color grading (high saturation, contrast)
  - Motion blur for speed
