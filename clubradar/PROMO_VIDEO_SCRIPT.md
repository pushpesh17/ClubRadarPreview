## **VISUAL STYLE GUIDE**

### **Color Palette**:

- Primary: Purple (#9333EA) and Pink (#EC4899) gradients
- Accent: Neon blues, electric greens for nightlife feel
- Background: Dark with neon accents
- Text: White/light colors for contrast

### **Typography**:

- Bold, modern sans-serif for headlines
- Clean, readable for body text
- Animated text reveals
- Glowing effects on key words

### **Animation Style**:

- Fast cuts (0.5-1 second per shot)
- Smooth transitions (fade, slide, zoom)
- Zoom effects on important elements
- Particle effects for energy
- Glitch effects for tech feel

### **Camera Movements**:

- Dynamic camera movements
- Handheld feel for authenticity
- Smooth tracking shots
- Quick pans and tilts
