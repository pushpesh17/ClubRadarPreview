## **PROMPT 1: Skip the Queue (8 seconds)**

### **Gemini Prompt**:
```
Create an 8-second video: Split screen showing long queue of people waiting outside dark club (left side, desaturated) vs person with glowing QR code on phone walking straight into bright, vibrant club (right side, colorful). Person enters smoothly while others wait. Dramatic contrast. Text overlay: "Skip the Queue" appears at 2 seconds. ClubRadar logo appears at 6 seconds. Fast-paced electronic music with bass drop at 4 seconds. Neon purple and pink lighting. Cinematic, high energy.
```

### **Visual Breakdown**:
- **0:00-0:02**: Split screen setup - queue vs QR person
- **0:02-0:04**: Text "Skip the Queue" appears, person walks past queue
- **0:04-0:06**: Bass drop, person enters club, queue still waiting
- **0:06-0:08**: ClubRadar logo appears, person celebrating inside

### **Text Overlays**:
- 0:02 - "Skip the Queue"
- 0:06 - "CLUBRADAR" logo

### **Voiceover** (Optional):
"While others wait... you enter. ClubRadar."
